About

In the mobile and game development communities alike, a quality user experience is a top priority for designers and developers. Playing a large variety of video games as a child exposed my mind to UX and usability concepts early on in life, eventually granting me the attention to detail and creative instinct that, with the skills of a software engineer, makes creating an effective experience possible.

My long standing passion for gaming led me to pursue an education that would give me the ability to bring my own ideas to life. Gaining experience delivering user friendly mobile solutions and a strong foundation in proper computer science along the way, earning my degree gave me the power to merge my creativity and capability to create engaging original applications.
